Plastic pollution : Over Usage of Plastics

Plastic pollution is the accumulation of plastic objects and particles in the Earth's environment that adversely affects wildlife, wildlife habitat, and humans.

This means that plastics gather in the sea that affects the marine wildlife.

Articles and Factual Informations.

1.Plastic Pollution

According to Charles Moore, Plastic pollution, accumulation in the environment of synthetic plastic products to the point where they create problems for wildlife and their habitats as well as for human populations.

2. They are everywhere - There’s just no getting away from microplastic contamination.

According to Cathleen O'Grady, microplastics may be having a moment in the spotlight, as the public is increasingly aware of their presence in the environment around us..

3. Microplastics are raining down from the sky.

According to Stephen Leahy, scientists discover large amounts of tiny plastic particles falling out of the air in a remote mountain location..
