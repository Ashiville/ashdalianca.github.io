PLASTIC POLLUTION : OVER USAGE OF PLASTIC

Our main problem is "Plastic Pollution: Over Usage of Plastic", and the people that has this problem is 27 out of 37 students in 7am-1 of Apec Schools. Plastic pollution is the accumulation of plastic objects and particles in the Earth's environment that adversely affects wildlife, wildlife habitat, and humans. This means that plastics gathered in the sea that affects the marine wildlife. Plastic pollution does not just affect marine wildlife, but also the different bodies of water. It also affects the waterways of low lying areas causing to numerous flood incidents because of plastics that are clogged in their waterways.

5 helpful tips on how to Reuse Plastic:

1. Create recycled plastic bottle supply cups;
2. Reuse coffee creamer containers for snack storage;
3. Make a DIY plastic bottle planter;
4. Upcycle laundry detergent bottles into a watering can;
5. Turn a milk carton into a garden scooper.

ENVIRONMENTAL RECYCLED BROOM-BROOM


The solution that we came up was called, Environmental Recycled Broom-Broom. Our Environmental Recycled Broom-Broom has a height of 81.5cm or 32in, the head of the broom has a length of 12.5cm or 5in, and the handle has a length of 63.5cm or 27.5in. We picked this as our solution because we think that this is tangible enough to reduce the plastic pollution. For us, our target participants will enjoy using it because it is very resplendent. PLASTIC POLLUTION: OVER USAGE OF PLASTIC

Background of the Study

1. Plastic Pollution - According to Charles Moore, Plastic pollution is the accumulation in the environment of synthetic plastic products to the point where they create problems for wildlife and their habitats as well as for human populations. By the end of the 20th century, plastics were found to be persistent polluters of some environmental niches. Sometimes they were mistaken as “food” by sea creatures. Plastics also clog the sewage system of low lying places causing the water to overflow and flood the streets.

2. They are everywhere - There’s just no getting away from microplastic contamination - According to Cathleen O'Grady, microplastics may be having a moment in the spotlight, as the public is increasingly aware of their presence in the environment around us. As more evidence of their presence comes to light, it becomes clear that we don’t know how big or small the problem is. A new paper in Nature Geoscience and some recent researches reports that microplastics could have travelled to some of the untouched parts of our world.

3. Microplastics are raining down from the sky - According to Stephen Leahy, scientists discover large amounts of tiny plastic particles falling out of the air in a remote mountain location. Researchers found out that in that remote mountain region, there were tiny bit of plastics raining down from the sky, raising questions about the global extent of plastic pollution. Scientists recorded a daily rate of 365 microplastic particles per square meter falling from the sky in the Pyrenees Mountains in France.

Environmental Recycled Broom Broom

How does the solution works?

1.Roll the cardboard and use a glue gun or double sided tape to paste.
2. Cover the cardboard with any paper and use a glue gun or double sided tape to cover.
3. Set aside the cardboard/handle
4. Proceed to the broom hair.
5.Get 5-8 empty water bottles and cut it horizontally
6. Paste the empty water bottles on the bottom part of the handle .

USABILITY TEST RESULT

Pre testing Summary Results
Based on our target participants ,all of them know some facts about Plastic pollution.In the second question, all of them also knows the causes of Plastic Pollution can affect our health. In the third question, all of them think that Plastic Pollution can affect our health. In the environment. And on the last question, they all gave tips or ways on how to stop the growing number of plastics.
Testing Summary Results
According to our participants, when they used our "Environmental Recycled Broom Broom ", sometimes it sweep's well but not all the time it can sweep well so with that response they said that the brooms's hair needs an improvement.
Post testing Summary Results
According to our target participants,on the first question, 90% of our participants says that the parts we need to improve is the broom's hair. On our second question, also 90% of our participants suggested new materials to use on the broom's hair. On the third question, 100% of our participants says that when we sell our product, a lot pof people will purchase it. On the fourth question, 99% of our participants say that we can earn profit from our solution, and only 1% said no. On our last question, 80% of the our participants can't think of another solutioin that suits our problem, while only 20% suggested another solution.
